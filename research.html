Research

Home / Resarch

At MIT, pushing the boundaries of knowledge and
possibility is our joyful obsession, and we celebrate
fundamental discoveries and practical applications alike.
As educators, we also value research as a potent form of
learning by doing.

Research flourishes in our 30 departments across five schools and

one college, as well as in dozens of centers, labs, and programs that convene
experts across disciplines to explore new intellectual frontiers and solve important societal problems. Our on-campus research capabilities are enhanced through the work of MIT Lincoln Laboratory, the Woods
Hole Oceanographic Institution, active research relationships with industry,
and a wide range of global collaborations.
